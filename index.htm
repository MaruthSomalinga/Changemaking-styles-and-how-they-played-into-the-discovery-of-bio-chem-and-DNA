This discovery took a while to be fully understood, Oswald Avery proved that the nucleic acid DNA carries genectic information, but nobody could understand it. Two groups of people were working on figuring out the structure of DNA, Watson and Francis were one group and the other was Franklin and Wilkins. Both groups collided when Franklins parter Wilkins showed Watson a piece of Franklins work called exposure 51, without her knowledge. After this knowledge Watson proposed that the DNA structure was made up of 2 nucleic acids winding up and down. They published this work in April 1953. They later received a nobel prize for their work, but Franklin was not credited.
